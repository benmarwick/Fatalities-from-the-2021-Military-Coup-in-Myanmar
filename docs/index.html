The data

The data come from the Assistance Association for Political Prisoners (Burma) (Wikipedia) and their fatality lists posted with their ‘daily briefing’ blog posts. They also post summaries to Twitter.

Related work

Similar data are presented on a Google Data Studio dashboard titled Myanmar Spring Revolution Fallen Stars. The dashboard does not list its sources.
